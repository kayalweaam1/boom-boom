Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Boom Boom (“the Game”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

1. Information We May Collect

We may collect certain information depending on the features you choose to use in the game.

A. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Gameplay progress, scores, items, and statistics
  • Crash logs and performance data
  • Anonymous analytics to improve gameplay and balance features

B. Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you use optional features such as creating an account, logging in with email or third-party services, cloud saving, leaderboards, or customizing your player profile, we may collect:

  • Name or nickname
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Approximate location (only with your permission)

We only collect this information if you choose to use a feature that requires it and you give consent.

2. How We Use the Information

  • Improve game performance and fix issues
  • Offer cloud saving and restore progress
  • Maintain leaderboards and rankings
  • Provide personalized features and player profiles
  • Prevent cheating and abuse
  • Display ads and provide in-game rewards
  • Enhance overall gameplay experience

We do NOT sell your data to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Boom Boom may use trusted third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads
  • Firebase Analytics – for analytics and performance insights
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash reports
  • Firebase Firestore – for cloud save features

These services may collect anonymous information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

Boom Boom is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe information was collected by mistake, you may contact us to request deletion.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss or misuse, and alteration. Any data transferred to third-party services is encrypted.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Disable personalized ads via device settings
  • Reset your game data at any time
  • Choose not to use features that require personal information
  • Request deletion of cloud-saved data (if applicable)

You are always in control of your information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in the game or on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
